Community Rulesقوانين المجتمع

01
Respect everyone. No hate speech, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
02
Keep topics relevant to each channel. Programming help goes in #programming-help, not general chat.
03
No spam, self-promotion, or advertising without moderator permission.

Cybersecurity discussions are for education only. No illegal activity, malware sharing, or real target discussions.

Help freely. The best way to learn is to teach. Be patient with beginners.
06
When sharing code, use code blocks. When asking for help, include your error message and what you've already tried.
